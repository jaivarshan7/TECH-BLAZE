Event Description

EXPLORE THE EXTREME

Tech Blaze – eXplore the eXtream is a dynamic technical symposium jointly organized by the Department of Computer Applications and the Department of Computer Science. This vibrant event served as a platform to showcase and celebrate the technical prowess of students from various institutions, as well as the talented minds from Hindustan Institute of Technology and Science (HITS).
The symposium brings together innovation, creativity, and competitive spirit through a range of technical events and challenges. It encourages collaboration, sharpens problem-solving skills, and highlights emerging trends in the world of technology. Tech Blaze stands as an inspiring forum where budding technologists can explore, express their abilities, and push the boundaries of excellence.
