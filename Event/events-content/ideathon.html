Event Structure
  • Join the event as a team! Teams of 2 to 4 members are encouraged to participate.
General Rules
  • Problem statements will be provided by the organizers or approved prior to the event.
  • Teams will be given one week to research, ideate, and develop their solution.
  • Each team must submit a problem-solution abstract within 2 days.
  • Each team must submit a final PowerPoint (PPT) within the specified deadline.
The judges’ and the event co-ordinators’ decision would be the final decision.
