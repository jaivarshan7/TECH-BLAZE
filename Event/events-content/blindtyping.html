TYPE WITH YOUR MIND, NOT YOUR EYES

Event Structure
  • Participation: Individual participation
  • Format: Computer-based event
  • Event Dates: 19-Feb-2026 to 25-Feb-2026
  • Winner Announcement: 27-Feb-2026
Rules
  • Participants must type the given text without viewing the actual content.
  • The text will contain a mix of alphabets, numbers, symbols, and random words.
  • No external tools, software, or assistance are allowed during the event.
  • Copy–paste actions are strictly prohibited.
  • Accuracy and typing speed will be considered for evaluation.
  • Only the text typed within the given time limit will be evaluated.
The judges’ and event coordinator’s decision would be the final.
