Event Structure
  • Team Size: Maximum of 2 members.
General Rules
  • Only registered participants will be allowed to compete.
  • Involvement in malpractice will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • MOBILE (Mandatory)
  • The difficulty level will vary according to the Sets.
  • Necessary resources will be provided by the organizers.

Set 1: Written Round

Objective: The primary goal of this first stage is to evaluate your fundamental technical knowledge through a traditional, focused assessment.

  • Format: Paper & Pen (Q/A)
  • Duration: 40 minutes
  • Mode: Offline

Set 2: Google Form

Objective: This round shifts away from pure memory and focuses on your ability to research, filter information, and solve problems quickly using digital tools.

Speed & Accuracy: To test how quickly and accurately you can find technical solutions.

  • Format: Google Form
  • Duration: 40 minutes

Set 3: Visual & Discuss

Objective: To evaluate collaborative problem-solving and pattern recognition. The goal is for the team to look at a complex or abstract visual, discuss the technical possibilities, and arrive at a single correct answer within a short time frame.

  • Format: Visuals, Discussion & Answering
  • Duration: 50 minutes
