Format
  • Category: Technology Quiz
  • Team Size: Maximum 2 participants per team
  • Mode: Offline (Kahoot-style)
Rules and Regulations
  • The event can be played individually or in teams (maximum 2 members).
  • Questions will be based on technology, computers, and general IT knowledge.
  • The quiz will be conducted offline using a digital format.
  • Each question will have a time limit — faster responses earn higher scores.
  • No use of external tools or application, or external help during the quiz.
  • Involvement in malpractice will lead to immediate disqualification.

Judging Criteria

  • Accuracy of Answers: Points are awarded only for correct responses.
  • Speed of Response: Faster answers earn higher scores.
  • Consistency Across All Rounds: Stable performance in every round improves the chances of winning.
The judges’ and event coordinators’s decision would be the final.
