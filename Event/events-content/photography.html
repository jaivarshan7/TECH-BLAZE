TURNING SECONDS INTO STORIES.

Event Structure
  • Participation: Individual Participant
General Rules
  • The photograph must strictly follow the given theme; off-theme photographs will be disqualified.
  • All submissions must be original and captured by the participant during the given time line.
  • Only basic edits (exposure, contrast, color correction, cropping) are allowed. Heavy manipulation or AI-generated content will be prohibited.
  • Entries will be judged on creativity and storytelling.
The judges’ and event coordinators’ decision would be the final.
