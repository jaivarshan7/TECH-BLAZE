Event Structure
  • Team Size: 2-4 members per team.
  • Rounds: 1 Round
Rules
  • No speaking or lip movement.
  • No writing, drawing, or spelling in the air.
  • No sound effects or pointing to objects/people directly.
  • Only gestures, expressions, and body language are allowed.
Format
  • Type: Guess the word with actions
  • Rounds: 1 round
  • Mode: Offline

Round 1: Act & Decode

  • Secret words will be provided in chits.
  • 10 seconds for preparation.
  • 90 seconds acting time.
  • Team guesses aloud.
  • The host will validate.
