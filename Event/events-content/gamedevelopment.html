WHERE IMAGINATION BECOMES PLAYABLE.

Event Structure
  • Team Size: Maximum of 2 participants per team
General Rules
  • The game must strictly follow the given theme.
  • The game must be original and developed during the event duration.
  • Games must be completed and submitted within the given time limit.
  • Incomplete or non-functional games will be disqualified.
  • Participants must submit the game build, source files (if asked), and a short description of gameplay and controls.
The judges’ and event coordinators’ decision would be the final.
