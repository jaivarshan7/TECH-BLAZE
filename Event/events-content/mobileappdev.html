Description

Mobile App Development involves designing, developing, and maintaining applications for mobile devices such as smartphones and tablets. It focuses on creating efficient, user-friendly, and secure mobile applications for Android and iOS platforms using modern tools, frameworks, and technologies.

Rules
  • The activity can be done individually or in teams (maximum 2 members).
  • Participants should focus only on mobile app development concepts.
  • Mobile phones, internet access, or external resources are not allowed during the activity.
  • The task must be completed within the given time limit.
  • Any form of plagiarism or misuse of resources will lead to disqualification.
  • Participants must follow the instructions given by the coordinators.
Judging Criteria
  • Concept Understanding: Clarity in mobile app development concepts.
  • Technical Accuracy: Correct use of tools, technologies, and methods.
  • Creativity & Design: Innovation and user-friendly app design.
  • Implementation Quality: Proper structure and functionality.
  • Overall Performance: Consistency and completeness of work.
The judges' and event coordinators' decision would be the final.
