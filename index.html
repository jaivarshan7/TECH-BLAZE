FAQ'S

What is TechBlaze ?

TechBlaze is a proposed technical symposium organized by the Department of Computer Applications and Computer Science at Hindustan Institute of Technology and Science.

Do I need to pay to participate ?

Yes, participation in TechBlaze is completely paid.

Can I participate in multiple events ?

Yes, students are allowed to participate in multiple events

Are there certificates for all participants ?

Yes, certificates will be provided to winners and all participants upon participation will receive a e-certificate.

is transport facility available ?

No, transport facilities are not available for the participants.
